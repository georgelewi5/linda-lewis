Modern House

A garden designed for leisure and entertaining, integrating front and rear gardens including a client request for a bocce court. Various settings were created to entertain friends, including feature steps overlooking the bocce area.

A water feature was given centre stage to catch the shifting light and reflect surrounding planting and a ‘woven’ series of paths linked different zones in the garden and enhanced the monochromatic aesthetic of the house.
