Biggin Hill

This sunny garden project started with a review of the boundaries, cutting back mature hedges in some areas and planting raised hornbeams in others to judiciously screen areas which were overlooked by neighboring properties.

Working with the clients to retain mature plant specimens, the scheme was renovated creating a contemporary feel with roses and grasses repeated throughout the scheme. As a result, the garden feels more private and the grasses offer a sense of movement and catch the light at different times of day.
