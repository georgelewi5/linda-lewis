Surrey Winery (Diploma Project)

An imaginary brief, creating a network of long distance paths linking vineyards in the South East of England, this boutique hotel would be a stop along the way. The masterplan covered themed woodland walks, with an emphasis on wellbeing and relaxation, wine tasting, function rooms and extensive outdoor areas zoned into ‘rooms’ for drinks or outdoor dining.

The design merged the geometry of the house and tendril-like paths and beds. An aromatic courtyard offers visitors the chance to relax by water, surrounding by a planting scheme inspired by the aromas and colors of wine. In other areas, the areas are informally divided with apple espaliers and vines to enhance the sense of ‘place’.
