ABOUT

Rural garden setting, Lingfield

The brief was to join together the house and existing home offices and garden buildings with a scheme which celebrated the wonderful rural outlook.

A courtyard was created between the house and the home offices with an enclosed, protected feel, planting close to the house was in a cottage garden style and the garden room seating area was surrounded with prairie-style planting beds which reflected the colours and movement of the field beyond.
